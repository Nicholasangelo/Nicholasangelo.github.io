Features

Whether you're a digital nomad, traveller, or road tripper, Dinofii helps you discover new destinations. Define your search area as either Earth, a country, or region of a country, then choose the type of place you're looking for from the Select Feature menu. When you click Search, Dinofii will randomly return a matching feature in the search area you defined, as well as the closest city to that feature. Every piece of information returned is linked to more information. The feature name, country name, and feature type are all linked to search for more information about themselves. The feature's latitude and longitude coordinates will take you to a map of that location. The closest city information will take you to a map with directions from the city to the feature. The wifi icon will display the number of hotspots found in the area if that information is available, and the globe icon next to the wifi symbol will take you to a map of all known wifi hotspots in the area.

Design

Using Html, Bootstrap, and CSS we have designed a minimal and user friendly interface.

We have created a simple search bar with prepopulated features that you can search or browse.

Results are dynamicly displayed on Bootstrap Cards and stack as you continue to search.

Displayed results come with links to map your destination, search for areas with high internet traffic and gather more informtaion.

APIs @ Play

GEONAMES

Finds global locations (points of interest, not cities) based on a single feature code.

EZCMD

Finds postal codes and nearby cities based on latitude and longitude coordinates.

WIGLY

Finds the number of WiFi Hotspots in a given postal code, including detailed mapping.
