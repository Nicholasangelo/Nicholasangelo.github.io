Nicholas Angelo Batten was born in New York, and raised in Virginia. He earned a degree in Art History from Virginia Commonwealth University with an emphasis in Aesthetics and Film Studies and a focus in Italian Language and Culture. Nicholas is an experienced Actor, Writer, Director and Songwriter.

As a user-focused Full Stack Developer with a background in narrative Arts he believes that the user and aesthetic experience are tantamount, and that the present and future of storytelling is programming, web development, and user driven media. Nicholas is a curator of the unique experience, focused on the individual to inspire to build a better cultural awareness, and community.
